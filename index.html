Who Was Blessed Carlo Acutis?

Blessed Carlo Acutis is most well known for being the patron saint of programmers and youth. He died of Luekemia cancer and was only 15 when he died. His last words were "To be always united with Jesus, this is my plan of life". He was a great person that cared for everyone espacially cared about Jesus and should become a saint




Prayer to Blessed Carlo Acutis


In the name of the Father and the Son and the Holy Spirit Amen

God, Our Father, provider for the poor, champion of the oppressed, defender of the widow and orphan, you called your servant, Blessed Carlo Acutis, to be an apostle of the Holy Eucharist and model of Christian youth and holy industry. Through the example of his life and virtue may we follow your Son, Jesus Christ, fulfilling his commandment of charity more closely and building up his Body which is the Church. Let the inspiration of Blessed Carlo Acutis inspire us to greater confidence in your love so we may continue his work in spreading your love for us in your Eucharistic Presence. We humbly ask that you glorify Blessed Carlo Acutis on earth according to the design of your holy will. Through his intercession, grant the favor I now present (make your request), through Christ our Lord, Amen.
