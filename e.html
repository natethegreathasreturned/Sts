St.Denis Introduction

St.Denis is one of the fourteen Holy Helpers. He is a martyr and what he did after is pretty interesting.


How St.Denis got martyred

St.Denis got martyred by getting beheaded, which in my opinion is the worst way to die

What did St.Denis do when he got beheaded?

St.Denis kept on walking for miles while preaching a sermon of repentance while being accompanied by Angels who made him braver than ever.

Prayer to St.Denis

In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit. Amen. O God, Who conferred Your saving Faith on the people of France through Your holy bishop and martyr Denis, and did glorify him before and after his martyrdom by many miracles. Grant us, through his intercession, that the Faith practiced and preached by him may be our light on the way of life, so that we may be preserved from all anxieties of conscience, and if by human frailty we have sinned, we may return to You speedily by true penance. Moreover, I beseech You to graciously grant me, through the merits and intercession of this powerful Helper, the petition which through them I humbly place before You, devoutly saying, “Thy will be done on earth as it is in Heaven.” Amen
